Floods upon the Dry Ground

An Account of the 1921-3 Revival Among the Scottish Fisherfolk

About This Book

This remarkable account tells the story of the 1921-1923 revival that swept through the fishing villages of Scotland. Jackie Ritchie documents the powerful move of God among the Scottish fisherfolk, a revival that transformed communities and left a lasting spiritual legacy.
