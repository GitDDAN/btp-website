The Christian in Complete Armour

William Gurnall (1616-1679)

About This Book

The Christian in Complete Armour is a classic Puritan work by William Gurnall, an English author and clergyman. Based on Ephesians 6:10-20, this extensive commentary on the spiritual armour of God provides practical guidance for Christian living and spiritual warfare.

These daily devotional readings are excerpts from Gurnall's masterwork, arranged for daily meditation and spiritual growth.
