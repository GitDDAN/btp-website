The Fruit of the Spirit

A study based upon the Greek words describing the Fruit of the Spirit

About This Course

This course is part of the Christian Life Program exploring The Ministry of the Holy Spirit in the New Testament Church. Through 10 comprehensive lessons, we examine the Fruit of the Spirit as described in Galatians 5:19-25.

Course Text: Galatians 5:19-25

"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, meekness, temperance: against such there is no law."

These lessons are especially suitable for teaching new Christians on the importance of living a holy life.
