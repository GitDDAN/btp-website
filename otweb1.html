Old Testament History

From Creation to the Maccabees

Course Overview

This course examines the historical background of the Old Testament. Archaeology and the historical record reveal the setting of the Old Testament narratives from Creation through to the intertestamental period.

All presentations are available as PDF files. PowerPoint versions are available upon request.
