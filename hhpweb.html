Holiness, Healing & Pentecost

History of the Pentecostal Movement

Course Overview

This course examines the history of the Pentecostal and Charismatic Movement, tracing its roots from the 19th century Holiness movement through the Azusa Street Revival and beyond.

Each lesson includes PDF presentations with detailed study notes. Click on any lesson to view the presentation.

Course Lessons

1

Holiness and Healing

Two lessons showing how the Pentecostal Movement developed from the Holiness movement in the 19th century
2

Azusa Street

Two lessons on the Azusa Street Revival - events leading up to Azusa Street and what happened there
3

Pentecost Spreads (UK)

Two lessons on the spread of Pentecost after Azusa Street
4

The Pentecostal and Charismatic Movement in the 20th Century

Four lessons looking at the ministries of Aimee Semple McPherson, Kathryn Kuhlman, William Branham, and Oral Roberts.
