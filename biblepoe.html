Poetic Books

Wisdom Literature - 5 Books (KJV)

The Poetic Books, also called Wisdom Literature, contain Hebrew poetry dealing with themes of suffering, worship, wisdom, and love. These five books use parallelism and figurative language to express profound truths about God and human experience.

1

Job

42 Chapters

A righteous man's suffering and God's sovereignty - exploring the mystery of why the righteous suffer.
