About This Course

This advanced course examines Greek words specifically associated with leadership in the New Testament. These studies explore the character qualities and motivations that Scripture identifies as essential for godly leadership. Understanding these terms helps believers evaluate leadership according to biblical standards rather than worldly measures of success.
