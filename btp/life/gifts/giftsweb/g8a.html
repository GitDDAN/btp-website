Lesson 8: Discerning of Spirits

The Ministry of the Holy Spirit in the New Testament Church

Course: The Gifts of the Spirit

Lesson 8

Lesson Title: Discerning of spirits

Lesson Text to another discerning of spirits; 1 Corinthians 12:7-11

Introduction: What is the gift of the Discerning of spirits?

Discerning Greek diakrisis which means to distinguish between.

It is the empowerment of the Holy Spirit to clearly distinguish spirits.

There is no gift called the deliverance from evil spirits.

Authority has been given to believers to cast out devils in the name of Jesus.

Discerning of spirits is sometimes necessary to know what spirit is influencing or controlling an individual.

Satan works in the dark.

It is difficult for the powers of darkness to work evil when the works come to light.

An evil spirit seeks anonymity to work evil without hindrance.

Evil spirits are like insects that always seek to hide in the dark.

When they are brought to light they seek the darkness.

If the devil comes as an angel of light he is hiding his true identity.

Once an evil spirit is recognised it will run for the darkness.

The word spirits is the Greek word pneuma which literally means wind or breath.

This word is used in reference to the Holy Spirit, the spirit or inner life of a person, an evil spirit, or an angelic being.

Discerning of spirits is the gift of the Holy Spirit that will reveal the nature of a spirit.

In regard to the Holy Spirit there should be no need for a special gift to recognise Him.

The gift is necessary to recognise evil spirits and sometimes to recognise the presence of angels who are sent as ministering spirits.

Angels are sent to help believers especially during times of difficulty or to help believers fulfil a particular task.

The gift of discerning of spirits is given so that we will know what is happening in the unseen spirit realm.

An insight into this world is given in Ezekiel 8:7-12.

Ezekiel chapter 8:7-12

Ezekiel was shown a secret place not far from the Temple (the place of worship).

He went into this secret place and saw wicked abominations.

It was a habitation of devils.

In this place he saw 70 elders worshipping the idols on the wall.

It was not the young men but the old men who were there.

The Lord said to Ezekiel this is what the elders are doing in the imagination of their minds.

These idols are described in Galatians 5 under the works of the flesh: immorality; occultism; hatred and murder; revelling.

The old men were not doing this openly they were doing it in the secret place of their minds.

Nobody could see it.

But through the imagination they were dwelling in this evil secret place.

Testimony: The danger of relaxing the guard on our thoughts.

It can be relaxing to let down the guard on our mind and enjoy the pleasure of sinful thoughts.

Preachers and Christian leaders have fallen into sin because they went into the devils secret place in the imagination of their mind.

Thoughts that begin with immorality can then be led by a wrong spirit into deeper areas of sin and into the ream of occultism.

It is therefore essential for believers who have entered into this secret place of the devil to run from it.

The devil always works in the dark, he hates the light.

If a Christian has become entangled in the sins of the imagination pornography etc. then he must first repent which means change our thinking then turn away from it and finally confess it which means do not hide the sin but openly talk to other believers about it.

A good way to do it is to talk to someone about Ezekiel 8:7-12 and express your personal problem.

When you

1) resist the temptation to sin in your mind and

2) resist the devil by not hiding your sin but

3) confessing it as you

4) fellowship with other believers

then the devil will flee from you in the same way that an insect that is revealed hiding under a rock will flee to the darkness.

There is freedom in confession but this is not secret confession that works in the dark, but where we confess to one another.

Confession in the light of Christian fellowship.

Main Points

1. The Gift of Discerning of Spirits in scripture

In the Old Testament

Elisha and his servant

2 Kings 6:17, And Elisha prayed, and said, LORD, I pray thee, open his eyes, that he may see. And the LORD opened the eyes of the young man; and he saw: and, behold, the mountain was full of horses and chariots of fire round about Elisha.

Discerning of angelic beings - Elisha prayed open his eyes and he saw a host of angels surrounding the Syrian army.

The eyes of the servant were opened so that he saw the unseen spirit realm.

In this instance the gift enabled the young man to see angels.

In the Gospels

Woman with a spirit of infirmity

Luke 13:10-13, And he was teaching in one of the synagogues on the Sabbath. And, behold, there was a woman which had a spirit of infirmity eighteen years, and was bowed together, and could in no wise lift up herself. And when Jesus saw her, he called her to him, and said unto her, Woman, you art loosed from your infirmity. And he laid his hands on her: and immediately she was made straight, and glorified God.

Sickness is sometimes caused by a spirit of infirmity.

Discerning of spirits enables the believer to recognise a spirit of affliction.

Jesus loosed the woman from the spirit of infirmity and immediately she could straighten her back.

This was deliverance and the effect was immediate.

The Boy with a deaf and dumb spirit, Mark 9:16-27.

A father brought his son to Jesus.

The boy could not speak (Mark 9:17).

The father knew the boy had an evil spirit (Mark 9:18).

Jesus called the spirit a deaf and dumb spirit, so obviously the boy could not hear either (Mark 9:25).

The spirit caused the boy to foam at the mouth; gnash with his teeth; and to pine which literally means to be like a withered branch (Mark 9:18). The spirit was destroying the boy physically.

The boy was suicidal because the spirit often caused him to throw himself into a fire to be burnt, or into water to drown (Mark 9:22).

The boy had become like this when he was a child.

The disciples were unable to deliver the boy.

The disciples at this time had a delegated authority but Jesus was the anointed One.

Jesus told the disciples this kind comes not out but by prayer and fasting.

Fasting is an aid to prayer.

Deliverance comes through the anointing and prayer.

From the time the Holy Spirit came at Pentecost in Acts 2 the disciples carried the same anointing as Jesus and were able to do the same works.

2. Testimonies concerning the operation of the gift.

Missions: Pastor Hsi

Pastor Hsi was a Chinese Pastor in the 19th century.

On one occasion he prayed for a woman with an evil spirit.

The woman was set free but the evil spirit followed Hsi to a man who had been dying but was recovering.

Hsi had, without thinking, given the spirit permission to follow him.

Within a short time after Hsi came into the presence of the sick man, he died.

Can a Christian come under the influence of an evil spirit?

Christians are mortal. We have weaknesses. This is why we must put on the whole armour of God to stand against the schemes of the devil.

We must be very careful what we do with our bodies and with our minds.

Paul wrote dont you know that your bodies are the Temple of the Holy Spirit

Women can become seriously ill because of the desire to be slim anorexia is a physical illness that is linked to a deceptive evil spirit.

Christians must avoid these things the same as anyone else.

Christians can come under the influence of an evil spirit when wrong emotions and desires have been maximised, and these take control of the will.

This can happen by misusing or not taking care of our bodies.

In these cases discerning of spirits works with a Word of Knowledge to reveal the reason the person came under the influence of an evil spirit.

Drug addiction is physical and mental. It is hard to overcome any addiction but physical addiction is easier to overcome than mental addiction.

The mental addiction is Satans stronghold, the realm of suggestion.

If a Christian experiments with drugs he or she is in danger of coming under the influence of an evil spirit.

Discerning angelic helpers:

Personal Testimony: As a very young Christian I went to speak with a lady who was dying of cancer.

I had gone before but I was a young Christian and left without leading her to Christ.

I felt so bad because I had not told her about salvation.

But this time when I left home to visit her I discerned the presence of an angelic being. There was an angel sent to help me, and I knew I would not fail this time.

The ladys name was Babs and we had worked at the same office together. She was only about 50 years of age but when I saw her lying in the bed she was thin, and looked like a very old woman

Her daughter was there and I shared my testimony with her and her daughter.

Everything went well that evening and I was able to lead the dying lady to the Lord.

After I prayed for her to receive Jesus as her Lord and Saviour she held my hand and said that her doctor could not understand why it was she had lived so long, but she said to me now I know.

Soon after this Babs went to be with the Lord.

3. Why is this gift necessary today?

The gift is needed today for the same reason as there are people who need deliverance just as they did in Jesus day.

The devil still seeks to kill; to steal; and to destroy (John 10:10).

There are spirits of infirmity that afflict people.

The gift of discerning of spirits will enable the believer to see that the person is afflicted not because of a sickness but because of a spirit of infirmity. A person who is sick needs gifts of healings but a person who has a spirit of infirmity needs deliverance.

The effect will be astounding because the person will be instantaneous set free.

It will appear as though a miracle has taken place but it is not the workings of miracles but deliverance from a spirit of infirmity.

There are spirits that cause depression and suicidal practices.

Young people who cut themselves deliberately need deliverance.

Depression is a modern day epidemic.

When depression is caused by a physical defect the person needs gifts of healings or workings of miracles.

But when there is no physical defect then the gift of discerning of spirits will reveal the nature of the depression.

Even in the Church, depression is a major problem.

Christians should not accept this because it is the destructive working of Satan.

Discerning of spirits will help believers in spiritual warfare.

Elisha prayed that the Lord would open his servants eyes. (2 Kings 6:17). When the Lord opened his eyes then his fear of the enemy was removed.

When our eyes are opened to the spirit realm we will see that one with God is truly a majority.

Hebrews 13:6, So that we may boldly say, The Lord is my helper, and I will not fear what man shall do unto me.

Note: When a man or a woman is delivered from an unclean spirit then it is essential for that person to be on fire for the Lord. (Matthew 12:43-45).

The unclean spirit can return with others making the persons condition even worse than before.

The person needs to walk in the Spirit (Galatians 5:16); fill the mind with the scriptures ( Colossians 3:16a); be filled with the Holy Spirit by speaking and singing the scriptures (Colossians 3:16b).

Summary: The gift of discerning of spirits is given so that we will know what is happening in the unseen spirit realm.

1)      It is necessary in order to bring deliverance to those who are bound.

2)      It provides awareness of angelic beings that are sent to help us.

3)      It provides awareness of evil spirits that seek to bring sickness, depression and suicidal tendencies.

4)      It empowers believers in spiritual warfare.
