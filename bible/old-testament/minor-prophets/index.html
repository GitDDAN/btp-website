Minor Prophets

The Twelve - 12 Books (KJV)

The Minor Prophets are called "minor" only because of their shorter length, not lesser importance. These twelve books, known in Hebrew tradition as "The Twelve," span several centuries and address diverse situations with messages of judgment, repentance, and restoration. Each book delivers God's powerful word to His people.
