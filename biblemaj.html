Major Prophets

Extended Prophetic Works - 5 Books (KJV)

The Major Prophets are called "major" because of their length, not their importance. These five books contain extensive prophecies about Israel, Judah, and the coming Messiah. They spoke God's word during times of crisis, calling the people to repentance and proclaiming both judgment and hope.

1

Isaiah

66 Chapters

The "Gospel Prophet" - rich Messianic prophecies, including the Suffering Servant of chapter 53.

Read Book
2

Jeremiah

52 Chapters

The "Weeping Prophet" - prophecies of Jerusalem's fall and the New Covenant promise.

Read Book
3

Lamentations

5 Chapters

Jeremiah's lament over Jerusalem's destruction - poems of grief yet hope in God's faithfulness.

Read Book
4

Ezekiel

48 Chapters

Visions of God's glory - prophecies from exile including the valley of dry bones and future temple.

Read Book
5

Daniel

12 Chapters

Faith in exile and apocalyptic visions - the lion's den, fiery furnace, and prophecies of kingdoms.
