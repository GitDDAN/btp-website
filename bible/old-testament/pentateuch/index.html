The Pentateuch

The Law of Moses - 5 Books (KJV)

The Pentateuch, also known as the Torah or the Law of Moses, comprises the first five books of the Bible. These foundational texts cover creation, the patriarchs, the exodus from Egypt, and the laws given to Israel. Click on any book to download the PDF.
