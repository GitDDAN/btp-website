🎯 Our Mission

The Bible Teaching Program (BTP) exists to equip believers with sound biblical teaching so they can grow in their faith and effectively teach others. Our vision is to see disciples making disciples across the world.

We believe that anyone can teach from our courses. You don't need a seminary degree or years of experience - you simply need to be willing to learn and share what you've learned. Teachers only need to be one lesson ahead of their class.

Our materials are designed for small group settings, making biblical education accessible in homes, churches, cell groups, coffee mornings, and anywhere people gather to learn God's Word.

💡 How the Program Works

1

Learn the Lesson

Study the lesson materials beforehand. You only need to stay one lesson ahead of your group.

2

Gather a Small Group

This can be a cell group, coffee morning, friends at home, or any informal gathering of believers.

3

Teach the Lesson

Use the lesson notes but try to include personal illustrations and real-life applications.

4

Allow Discussion

Create space for questions, sharing, and meaningful discussion about the material.

5

Schedule Next Meeting

Arrange the time for your next lesson and encourage group members to invite others.

6

Multiply Leaders

Encourage group members to start their own groups, creating a multiplication of teachers.

✨ Benefits of the Bible Teaching Program

Learn While Working

It allows people to be in work and yet receive training that is of more practical benefit than studying at a residential Bible school.

Develop New Leaders

It gives responsibility of leadership to many who would never otherwise be in a leadership position.

Resource Database

Leaders become a database providing information for others to teach rather than doing all the teaching themselves.

Immediate Encouragement

It immediately encourages people to teach as it is only necessary to be one lesson ahead of the class.

Growth & Reproduction

It produces growth, ministries and reproduction as believers learn to teach others who in turn teach more.
