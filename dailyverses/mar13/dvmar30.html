Repentance brings us to Christ; faith alone unites us with Christ.

Jesus the Son of God became sin for us on the cross;

He passed through the judgment on our behalf;

He took our guilt and condemnation;

He removed it as far as the east is from the west.

We are crucified with Christ and risen with Him;

no longer condemned sinners but inheritors of the promises of God.

In Him we have entered into the glorious freedom of new life.

We are sons and daughters of the living God; Christ lives in us;

and we know we shall be with Him forever.
