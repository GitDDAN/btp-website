Worship Course 2

The Heights of Anointed Worship

7 Lessons on Deeper Worship

Course Aim

That the student will understand there are higher realms of anointed worship that we should desire and seek after. This course explores the biblical foundations of Spirit-led worship and how believers can experience deeper communion with God through worship.

📖
"But the hour is coming, and is now here, when the true worshippers will worship the Father in spirit and truth, for the Father is seeking such people to worship him. God is spirit, and those who worship him must worship in spirit and truth."
- John 4:23-24 (ESV)

📚 Course Lessons

11

God Taking Control in Worship: The Anointing

Understanding how God's presence takes over in true worship
12

Rejoicing in Persecution

How worship sustains us through trials and difficulties
13

Rejoicing in the Wisdom and Power of God

Worshipping God for His infinite wisdom and mighty power
14

Worshipping with Joy in Eternity

The eternal nature of worship and our heavenly destiny
15

The Desire of Worship: That I Might Know Him

Pursuing intimate knowledge of God through worship
16

The Great Symphony of Worship

Understanding corporate worship as a unified expression
17

Worshipping in the Holy Spirit

The role of the Holy Spirit in authentic worship
