The Life of John Bunyan

Author of The Pilgrim's Progress (1628-1688)

About John Bunyan

John Bunyan (1628-1688) was an English writer and Puritan preacher best known as the author of The Pilgrim's Progress. Born in Elstow, Bedfordshire, Bunyan's spiritual journey from a troubled youth to a powerful preacher and author makes for compelling reading.

This account traces his life from before his conversion through the painful exercises of his mind and his severe temptations. He is buried at Bunhill Fields in London.
