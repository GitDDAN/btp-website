How to Pray

by R.A. Torrey - A comprehensive study on the principles and practice of effective prayer

About This Book

R.A. Torrey's classic work on prayer provides practical instruction on how to pray effectively. This comprehensive 12-chapter study covers the fundamental principles of prayer, teaching believers how to approach God with confidence and see their prayers answered.

About the Author

Reuben Archer Torrey (1856-1928) was an American evangelist, pastor, educator, and writer. He was a close associate of D.L. Moody and served as the first superintendent of the Moody Bible Institute.
