The Gifts of the Spirit

The Ministry of the Holy Spirit in the New Testament Church

About This Course

A comprehensive study of the Gifts of the Spirit in scripture and their relevance for today. Learn about the nine spiritual gifts mentioned in 1 Corinthians 12:7-10 and how they operate in the life of believers and the church.

Course Goal

That the believer will desire to move in the gifts of the Spirit and will see the manifestations of the Holy Spirit in his or her life for guidance, to overcome the enemy, and to glorify the Lord.

Course Lessons

1

Manifestations of the Spirit

Understanding the purpose and function of spiritual gifts in the church
2

Word of Wisdom

The supernatural impartation of divine wisdom for specific situations
3

Word of Knowledge

Supernatural revelation of facts unknown to the natural mind
4

Faith

The supernatural gift of faith that moves mountains
5

Gifts of Healings

The supernatural power to heal various kinds of sickness and disease
6

Working of Miracles

Supernatural acts of power that override natural laws
7

Prophecy

Speaking forth the mind and counsel of God for edification and comfort
8

Discerning of Spirits

Supernatural insight to distinguish between spirits
9

Kinds of Tongues

The supernatural ability to speak in languages not learned
10

Interpretation of Tongues

The supernatural ability to interpret messages given in tongues
