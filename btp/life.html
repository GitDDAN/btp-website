Christian Life Course

This course provides practical biblical teaching for living the Christian life in the power of the Holy Spirit. Learn about spiritual gifts, the fruit of the Spirit, prayer, worship, and spiritual warfare.

Course Overview

New Testament Christianity is the ministry of the Holy Spirit through the body of Christ. This course equips believers to walk in the Spirit and fulfill their calling in the church.

The Holy Spirit in the New Testament Church

The Fruit of the Holy Spirit

Teaching on the fruit of the Holy Spirit (Galatians 5:22-23).

The fruit of the Spirit is contrasted with the works of the flesh and examined in detail.

The Ministry of the Holy Spirit in the New Testament Church

Based on John 7:37-39.

This course shows how the ministry of the Holy Spirit has not changed. New Testament Christianity is the Ministry of the Holy Spirit through the body of Christ.

The Gifts of the Holy Spirit

Teaching examining the gifts of the Holy Spirit (1 Corinthians 12:8-10).

Prayer

The Importance of Effective Prayer

The essential nature of prayer in the body of Christ.

The Different Kinds of Prayer

An exposition of the different forms of prayer.

The Armour of God

Exposition of the Armour of God

Teaching on Ephesians 6:10-17 showing the need to be prepared for spiritual warfare.

Worship

The Life of Worship

The Christian Life is a life of worship. An introductory course into the life of worship.

The Heights of Anointed Worship

Reaching the heights of anointed worship.

This course shows how the highest levels of worship are the place of anointed worship, and the highest level of worship is also the place of intercessory prayer.
