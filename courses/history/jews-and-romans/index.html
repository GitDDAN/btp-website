Jews and Romans

Jewish History (37 BC - AD 135)

Course Overview

This course examines the historical background from the time of Herod the Great until the Bar Kokhba Revolt (37 BC - AD 135). Understanding this period is essential for grasping the political and social context of the New Testament.

Each lesson includes PDF presentations with accompanying notes for deeper study.

ğŸ“ Historical Context

This period covers the entire New Testament era - from the birth of Christ under Herod the Great, through the ministry of Jesus and the apostles under Roman procurators, to the destruction of Jerusalem in AD 70 and the final Jewish revolt under Bar Kokhba.
