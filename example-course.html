About This Course

The Foundation Course is designed to establish believers in the fundamental truths of Christianity. This comprehensive study covers essential doctrines including salvation, the nature of God, the authority of Scripture, and the work of the Holy Spirit.

Whether you're a new believer or looking to strengthen your understanding of core biblical principles, this course provides a solid biblical framework for spiritual growth.
