8 Types of Opposition in Serving the Lord

The book of Nehemiah tells us exactly what we are likely to come against in serving the Lord. The walls were broken down and the gates burnt. Nehemiah faced eight distinct types of opposition as he rebuilt the walls of Jerusalem ‚Äî the same opposition we face today in our spiritual work.
