The Armour of God

Equipping believers to stand against spiritual attacks - Ephesians 6

About This Course

Ten comprehensive lessons on the Christian Armour from Ephesians 6. These studies are especially suitable for teaching young people on the essential need of being equipped to stand against the attacks of the devil.

Course Focus

Young Christians and experienced leaders all need to be ready at all times, equipped with every piece of armour. Satan seeks opportunity to destroy our testimony, our marriage, or our ministry.
