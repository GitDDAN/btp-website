Athenian Democracy


a)Governing body - supreme 'assembly':

Ekklesia of all citizens - open to all male citizens over 18.

Met in amphitheatre in Pnyx which could hold 6000,

Met minimum 40 times a year (4 times a month).

Each citizen had the right to speak.

Majority vote decided issues of war and peace, treaties, finance, legislation, public works, all government activity.

Supreme in all legislative.

b)'Council of 500 '; Boule, chosen by lot; steering committee.

Boule had a president. Changed every meeting no one could be president more than once.

c)Law courts:dykasteria - from dike, justice:201-501 jurors/ judges who were chosen by lot. There was no distinction between judges and jurors.
