Greek Words (Part 2)

Leadership in the New Testament

This course examines the meaning of Greek words associated with leadership in the New Testament. Understanding these words helps us grasp the biblical model for Christian leadership.

Available Lessons

11

Kingly Character - 'semnos'

The meaning of the word semnos translated "grave" or "honest" in the New Testament
12

Contention - 'eritheia'

The contrast between right and wrong motivation in leadership. Contention arises from self-seeking ambition, but when love motivates there will be peace.
13

Godliness - 'eusebeia'

Eusebeia describes the Christian life as a life of worship - the expression of love flowing from the life of Christ in the believer.
