History of Modern Missions

The Spread of the Gospel Worldwide

Course Overview

This course traces the history of Protestant missions from the background of European expansion through to missions in the United Kingdom. Learn about the pioneers who carried the gospel to the nations and the movements that transformed world Christianity.

Each lesson includes PDF presentations with detailed study notes. Click on any lesson to view the presentation.
