About Missions

The Great Commission calls every believer to participate in God's mission to reach the world with the gospel of Jesus Christ. This course explores biblical foundations for missions, the role of the church in world evangelization, and practical aspects of missionary work.

Learn about God's heart for the nations, the history of missions, and how you can fulfill your calling in advancing the kingdom of God to the ends of the earth.
