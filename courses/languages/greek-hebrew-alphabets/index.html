Biblical Alphabets

Hebrew & Greek - The languages of Scripture

Course Overview

This course introduces you to the alphabets used in the original biblical languages. The Hebrew alphabet (22 letters) was used to write the Old Testament, while the Greek alphabet (24 letters) was used for the New Testament.

Learning these alphabets is the first step toward deeper Bible study and understanding word meanings in their original languages.
