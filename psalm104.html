Psalm 104: Bless the LORD, O My Soul

Commentary on creation and God's majesty

About This Study

Psalm 104 is one of the most beautiful and majestic psalms in Scripture, celebrating God as Creator and Sustainer of all things. This psalm provides a powerful meditation on the glory of God displayed in His creation, from the heavens above to the depths of the sea.

The psalmist declares "Bless the LORD, O my soul! O LORD my God, You are very great" and proceeds to describe God's power and wisdom in creating and maintaining the natural world. This study explores the theological and practical implications of worshiping God as the sovereign Creator of all things.
