Christian Life Course

Practical teaching on living the Christian life - from the ministry of the Holy Spirit to spiritual warfare

About This Course

Learn practical principles for spiritual growth, discipleship, and living out your faith in daily life. This comprehensive course covers the ministry and work of the Holy Spirit, spiritual gifts, prayer, spiritual warfare, and worship.

Course Focus

Equipping believers to walk in the power of the Holy Spirit and live victoriously in Christ.

Course Modules

1

The Fruit of the Holy Spirit

A study based on the Greek words describing the Fruit of the Spirit
2

The Ministry of the Holy Spirit

The work of the Spirit in the New Testament Church (John 7:37-39)
3

The Gifts of the Holy Spirit

Understanding and operating in spiritual gifts
4

The Importance of Effective Prayer

Learning to pray with faith and power
5

The Different Kinds of Prayer

Exploring various types and methods of prayer
6

The Armour of God

Exposition of spiritual warfare from Ephesians 6
7

The Life of Worship

Living a life of worship to God
8

The Heights of Anointed Worship

Deeper dimensions of Spirit-led worship
