The Higher Calling to Worship

Understanding that the highest calling is the call to worship

About This Course

This course explores the highest calling of every believer - the call to worship. Learn how to live a life of continuous worship and draw near to God's presence.

Course Aim

For the Christian to understand that the highest calling is the call to worship and that he or she will live a life of continuous worship.

The poem 'Worshippers' expresses the message of this course.
