About This Course

Build a solid foundation in core Christian doctrines including salvation, the cross, eternal life, and fundamental biblical truths. This course is designed for new believers and those wanting to strengthen their understanding of essential Christian teachings.

Course Focus

Understanding the foundational truths of the Christian faith that every believer should know.

Salvation

2

The Cross

The power and significance of Christ's sacrifice on the cross
3

The deity and eternal nature of our Lord Jesus Christ
