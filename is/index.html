About This Collection

Isaac Sharp was a Quaker missionary who traveled to the remote Faroe Islands and Iceland in 1862. His journal provides a fascinating account of early missionary work in these northern lands.

These historical documents offer insight into the dedication and faith of 19th century missionaries.
