Isaac Sharp's 1862 Mission Journey

Isaac Sharp and Asbjorn Kloster in the Faroe Islands (April June 1862)

Journal of Isaac Sharp - May 21, 1862

Friends assembling in London to-day, for the first sitting of the Yearly Meeting, were in frequent remembrance.

Funningur

Elduvik

At Eidevig(Elduvik), whither we next went, about sixty-two came together on a short notice; and at half-past six in the evening, a meeting was held in a large upper-room at the house of ClementHogneson, by whom and his wife we were very kindly cared for.
