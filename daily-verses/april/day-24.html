What we talk about will show what is on our heart.

When Christ fills our heart then our words

will be filled with thankfulness for all He has done for us.

The mighty God willingly gave Himself for us;

freely gives us new life;

sets us free from the power of sin,

and has brought us into the family of God.

Our rightful response

is to have a heart filled with thankfulness and joy.

Why should we wallow in the cesspit of the world

when we have been lifted to the heights of His glory.

We are royalty in Christ; we are not of the world; Christ lives in us.
