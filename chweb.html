Church History & the NT Standard

From the 1st Century to the Reformation

About This Course

An introduction to church history from the 1st century until the Reformation. This course examines how the early church developed, the challenges it faced, and the gradual departure from New Testament standards over the centuries.

Featured Scripture

"Unto him be glory in the church by Christ Jesus throughout all ages, world without end. Amen." - Ephesians 3:21
