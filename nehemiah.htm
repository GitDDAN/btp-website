NEHEMIAH

The book of Nehemiah tells us exactly what we are likely to come against in serving the Lord.

Walls were broken down, the gates burnt

2. Opposition through ridicule 4:1-6

3. Opposition through the threat of attack 4:7-9

4. Opposition through discouragement 4:10

5. Opposition through extortion 5:1-13

6. Opposition through compromise 6:1-4

7. Opposition through slander 6:5-9

1. Opposition through being despised 2:19-20
