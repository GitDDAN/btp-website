Isaac Sharp

1806 - 1897

English Quaker who journeyed to the remote Faroe Islands and Iceland in 1862. His journal provides a fascinating account of early missionary work in the northern lands.

About the 1862 Mission

Isaac Sharp and Asbjorn Kloster undertook a remarkable missionary journey to the Faroe Islands and Iceland in 1862. Their travels brought the Gospel to remote northern communities and documented the spiritual conditions of these isolated lands.

These historical documents offer invaluable insight into the dedication and faith of 19th century missionaries, as well as a fascinating glimpse into life in the Faroe Islands and Iceland during this period.
