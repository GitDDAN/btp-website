About The Pilgrim's Progress

The Pilgrim's Progress is a Christian allegory written by John Bunyan while imprisoned for preaching without a license. First published in 1678, it has been translated into more than 200 languages and has never been out of print.

The story follows Christian on his journey from the "City of Destruction" to the "Celestial City." Along the way, he encounters memorable characters and locations that represent various aspects of the Christian life and spiritual warfare.
