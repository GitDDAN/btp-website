Ephesians
"According to the eternal purpose which he accomplished in Christ Jesus our Lord:"

Ephesians 3:11

Devotional Thought

The eternal purpose of God is far more than forgiveness of sins.

In Christ we have been given His life so we serve as His body.

We have become His family;

filled with His love.

We are seated with Him; a victorious and blessed people.
