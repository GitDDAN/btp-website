The Christian in Complete Armour

Daily devotionals by William Gurnall

About This Classic Devotional

William Gurnall (1617-1679) was an English Puritan pastor who authored The Christian in Complete Armour, one of the most comprehensive and beloved works on spiritual warfare in Christian literature. This masterpiece provides deep exposition of Ephesians 6:10-20, examining each piece of the Christian's spiritual armor in detail.

Below are daily devotional readings from this timeless work, covering the topic of prayer in the Spirit. Each entry offers profound spiritual insight and practical application for the believer's daily walk with God.
